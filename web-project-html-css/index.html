JobSeek is a websource where it would make it easier for all who are searching for employees, and who are looking for a suitable job to be employed at.
Our site offers beneficial services for both employers who are recruiting candidates, and for job seekers who are in search for a job that best fits their qualifications.
For further details, please review our list of services.
To get started with our site, click here.
For more info on our website, please contact our customer services.

The Job Seek website started off as a graduation project by Giorgio Murad in 2024, and was soon taken advantage by a number of non-employers who are looking for jobs around the country.
The web application was later introduced and published live, and gained significant popularity in Lebanon, and being labeled as one of the top Lebanese employment site until the web-application was fully globalised.

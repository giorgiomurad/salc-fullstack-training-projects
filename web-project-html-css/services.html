Services

  1. Services and Benefits for Job Seekers:
    • Simple & reliable connection and communication.
    • Allowing Job Seek users to sign up as candidates looking for work.
    • Application to fill out with candidates information, qualifications, and experience.
    • The more experience a candidate has, the better chance for the user's profile to be suggested to current employer users looking for employees.
    • Users can stay up-to-date and notified of newly added job-offer that fits user's criteria
    • Allowing users to directly contact employers through our messaging service, or through the employer's provided contact information.
  2. Services and Benefits for employers:
    • Allowing employers to sign up as employers looking for employees.
    • Employers can provide information and general idea about their organization, and provide details about the purpose and services that the company offers.
    • Employers can list one or more job offer with information of required employee qualification and experience, and displaying on the organization's profile.
    • Allowing employers to search, filter, and view employees currently looking for work.
    • Allowing employers to directly communicate with employees through our messaging service, or through employee's provided contact info.
